Skill-shot

AR Android Game - NYP SIDM Open house

Made in 3 days, the AR game was made to replace common carnival games, and to introduce visitors to the School of Interactive and Digital Media and the courses they offer.

vuforia with Unity, this game was made for Android devices and features realistic ball and shooting physics.

The game was made by a 2-men team. Chris, the artist and myself, the programmer.

You play this by swiping the phone just like in Pokemon Go and try to get the balls into the holes, the smaller the holes, the more the points.
